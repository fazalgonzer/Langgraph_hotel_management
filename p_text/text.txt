Hotel Booking Policies

    Reservation Policy:
        Reservations can be made through our website, mobile app, or customer service hotline.
        A valid credit card is required to guarantee your reservation. Your card will not be charged until check-in, unless specified otherwise.

    Cancellation Policy:
        Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours prior to the scheduled check-in date to avoid a cancellation fee, which may equal one night's stay.
        No-shows will incur a charge of one night's stay.

    Check-In and Check-Out:
        Check-in time is from 3:00 PM. Early check-in may be available upon request but is not guaranteed.
        Check-out time is 11:00 AM. Late check-out requests may be accommodated based on availability and may incur an additional fee.

    Room Policies:
        Guests are responsible for the condition of the room during their stay. Any damages or excessive cleaning required may incur additional charges.
        Smoking is prohibited in all rooms and common areas. Violation of this policy may result in a cleaning fee.

    Age Requirement:
        Guests must be at least 18 years old to check in. Valid identification will be required upon arrival.

    Payment Policy:
        Payment is due at check-in. We accept major credit cards, debit cards, and cash.
        A security deposit may be required at check-in and will be refunded at check-out if no damages are incurred.

    Pet Policy:
        Pets are allowed in designated pet-friendly rooms for an additional fee. Please notify the hotel in advance if you plan to bring a pet.

    Room Availability:
        Room availability is subject to change and may vary based on demand. It is recommended to book in advance to secure your desired room type.

    Special Requests:
        Special requests (such as room location or amenities) will be noted but cannot be guaranteed. We will do our best to accommodate your needs.

    Customer Support:
        Our customer service team is available 24/7 to assist with any inquiries or issues during your stay. You can reach them through our website or by calling the hotel directly.

FAQs (Frequently Asked Questions)

    How do I make a reservation?
        You can make a reservation through our website, mobile app, or by calling our customer service.

    What is your cancellation policy?
        Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours before check-in to avoid a fee.

    What time is check-in and check-out?
        Check-in is from 3:00 PM, and check-out is at 11:00 AM.

    Can I bring my pet?
        Yes, pets are allowed in designated rooms for an additional fee. Please inform us in advance.

    What forms of payment do you accept?
        We accept major credit cards, debit cards, and cash at check-in.
